Instructions

Movement and interactions

You can move up, down, left, and right using the arrow keys, and interact with objects using the spacebar.

You can interact with objects by facing them and pressing spacebar. Here are some examples:

  • You can pick up onions by facing an onion dispenser or an onion on a counter and pressing spacebar.
  • If you are holding an onion and are facing an empty counter, and press spacebar, you will put the onion on the counter.
  • If you are holding an onion and are facing a pot that is not full, and press spacebar, you will put the onion in the pot.


Note that as you and your partner are moving around the kitchen you cannot occupy the same location.



Cooking

Once 3 onions are in the pot, the soup will automatically start cooking.

When a soup begins cooking, you'll notice a red number appear over the pot to denote the cook time. This number counts upward until the soup is done. Soups take 20 timesteps to cook.

Each soup is worth 20 points.



Goal

The team will be awarded 20 points for each soup served. Your goal in this task is to serve as many soups as you can before each level ends.
